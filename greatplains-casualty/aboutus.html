About Us

Who We Are

Great Plains Casualty is an admitted property & casualty insurance carrier domiciled in the State of Iowa. We provide high-quality, convenient and affordable insurance solutions to the transportation industry nationally via an efficient digital business model.

We specialize in serving bothowner-operators of heavy trucks operating over the road under a lease agreement, and contract carriers operating under their own authority in the final mile/home delivery segment of the transportation market.

  • Founded in 2007
  • Based in Cedar Rapids, Iowa
  • Licensed in all states and the District of Columbia, Aexcept for Hawaii
  • AM Best rating of A- (Excellent)

How GPC is Different

Our roots in the transportation industry give us deeper insight into what owner operators and contract carriers need to protect their business. We’re proud to offer an innovative, customer-centric approach that makes long approval times and one-size-fits-all coverage a thing of the past.
