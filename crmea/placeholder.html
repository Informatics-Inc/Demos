Our Initiatives

Planting Roots For Healthier Lives

We want everyone in Cedar Rapids to have access to fresh food, and the opportunity to grow it themselves.

Matthew 25 operates a 2-acre urban farm, a weekly urban produce market, a Community Supported Agriculture (CSA) subscription program and community gardens, all designed to bring fresh, quality produce to areas of the city that need it most.

Our Stories

Our Mission-Based Market and Cafe

We offer free or reduced food options for those in-need. Groundswell Café is a pay-it forward location where guests can round up their bill or donate to help pay for a meal. Our Cultivate Hope Market in Cedar Rapids’ Time Check neighborhood sells produce at half-price for qualifying individuals.
