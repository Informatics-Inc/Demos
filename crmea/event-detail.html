Oktoberfest BizMix hosted by Hilton Garden Inn

Thursday, October 20, 2022
4:00 PM - 6:00 PM CST

Hilton Garden Inn
4640 North River Blvd. NE
Cedar Rapids, IA 52411

Join us for the October BizMix, hosted by Hilton Garden Inn!

This month's BizMix will be Oktoberfest themed. Food and beverages will be provided. We hope to see you there!

BizMix brings together area professionals and is hosted by a different member business each month. This event provides EA members with increased opportunity for participation and connections.
