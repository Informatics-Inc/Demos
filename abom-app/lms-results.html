Your Answers

Question: Idiopathic condylar resorption (ICR) is a pathologic remodeling of the mandibular condyles. Which of the following is true regarding (ICR)?

Answer: Most patients are women in the 15 to 35 yr. age bracket

Question: A common complication of conventional orthognathic surgical treatment of the open bite deformity resulting from (ICR) is:

Answer: post- surgical relapse.

Question: When comparing idiopathic condylar resorption (ICR) to juvenile idiopathic arthritis (JIA) which of the following statements are true?

Answer: JIA will show significant changes in inflammatory markers such as RF and C-reactive protein, while ICR will not.
