EMS Training

Driving the Future of Ambulance Care

Area Ambulance Service is proud to play a part in training the next generation of EMS professionals in Eastern Iowa.

From ride-alongs for paramedic students to our new comprehensive EMT academy, we are dedicated to creating the skilled medical professionals our region needs. Learn more about our programs, find student info, or consider supporting our work with a charitable gift.

Why Train with Area Ambulance

We are the training partner of choice for paramedics and EMTs who want to be leaders in their field.

The Best Mentors

Learn in the field with the state’s most experienced paramedics and EMTs. Our trainees aren’t just driving the truck—they’re called to be hands-on contributors at every call.

The Hardest Calls

Join a training program that prepares you for the real challenges of EMS. Area Ambulance handles the region’s most calls and the most complex cases, giving you unrivaled experience.

The Newest Equipment

Learn on the job with the best gear, from automated CPR machines to advanced patient lift systems.
