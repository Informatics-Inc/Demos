Join a team that’s setting the standard for excellence in emergency medical services.

At Area Ambulance Service, you’ll work alongside some of the best EMTs and paramedics in the state, in an organization that actually has your back. If you’re a driven paramedic or EMT looking for a more challenging job, we’ve got it.

What We Offer

Great Equipment

At Area Ambulance Service, we invest in the tools you need to do your job safely and effectively. From new ambulances to advanced technology, we ensure our teams have the resources they need to perform at their best.

Great Facilities

Our facilities are designed to keep you prepared, comfortable, and focused. Our Cedar Rapids headquarters features modern training facilities and a fully-equipped gym, and a new Marion satellite location is coming in 2025.

Great Culture

At Area Ambulance Service, we pride ourselves on fostering a culture of ownership and initiative. Whether it’s joining a committee or leading a project, we support team members who want to raise the bar.

The Rewards are Real

Working at Area Ambulance Service isn’t just about service—we’re proud to offer benefits and amenities you don’t expect from a not-for-profit.

Financial Benefits

We’re proud to offer competitive wages, a retirement match, and ultra-low health care premiums.

Generous PTO & Events

You need time and support to recharge, which is why we offer a robust PTO program, appreciation events, and more.

Career Development

Hone your EMS skills with high volumes and complex cases that build confidence.
