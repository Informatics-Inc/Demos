Ambulance Service You Can Trust

Area Ambulance Service is your lifeline to medical care in Cedar Rapids, Marion, and 15 surrounding communities.

Covering 250 square miles, our team of 75 dedicated paramedics and EMSs ensures every call is met with skill, compassion, and the latest EMS technology. As an independent nonprofit, we are focused solely on serving the public and providing exceptional care when it matters most.

  • 17 Advanced Life Support ambulances
  • 500 Combined years of EMS Experience
  • 30,000 Calls handled annually
