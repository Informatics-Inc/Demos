Inside The Ride

See the Tech Powering Our Ambulances

The ambulances you see on the streets of Linn County aren’t just trucks—they are state-of-the-art mobile medical clinics designed to maximize patient outcomes.

Each truck operated by Area Ambulance Service represents a half-million dollars of investment, and features the latest in lifesaving equipment. From automated CPR devices that free up paramedics to wireless technologies that allow crews to securely transmit patient data to hospital care teams, AAS ambulances provide the advanced care you expect for your loved ones when time is of the essence.

Learn more about the advanced equipment you’ll find in Area Ambulance units operating across Linn County.
