What do orthodontists do?

Team members at Hospitality Dental & Orthodontics assess the state of your teeth and determine which treatment is right for you. To do so they complete:

  • A full medical and dental history overview
  • A thorough dental exam
  • X-rays of teeth and jaw
  • Plaster models of your teeth
