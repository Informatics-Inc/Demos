Inside Our Design Process

Wondering how we take a custom ring from start to finish?
Here’s the basic workflow.

Ideation

This is where our designers interpret your vision. We’ll hand sketch and make 3-D computer models of your ring, and select diamonds.

Refinement

We’ll make a wax model of your ring to review in person. If that looks good, we begin custom production.

Finishing

We’ll cast, assemble, engrave and polish the ring in house, presenting you with a beautiful custom piece for your loved one.
