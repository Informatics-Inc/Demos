The Philip's Story

What started as a small jewelry store in Cedar Rapids’ old Lindale Plaza (now Lindale Mall) in 1976 has grown into one of the region’s leading custom jewelry, repair and engraving studios.

Owner Phil High started at that operation, founded by his father, until opening his own retail store in 1983 in the Marion Square Plaza, and moving into their X,XXX-square-foot studio in 2014. There’s been plenty of change along the way.

The Philip's Philosophy

“We’re not like everyone else. We’re the little weird place, in the little town. A guy might say, “I’ll just send it to a big place in New York,” but you’re going to give it to the guy who gives it to the guy. When you lose control of the process, you can’t control the outcome.”

Phil High, Owner, Philip’s Diamond Shop
