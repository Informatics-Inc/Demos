Custom Ring

Abbey

14 karat white gold semi-mount “Abbey” engagement ring containing thirty-eight round brilliant ideal cut diamonds weighing 0.91 carats total weight with G-I color and SI clarity. Bezel set on each side of the profile of the center head are two round brilliant ideal cut diamonds weighing 0.14 carats total weight with G-I color and SI clarity. The ring features a Euro-shank with a 0.01 carat diamond and the Philip’s logo. The ring has been custom made by Philip’s Diamond Shop.

This item is built to order and can be modified to fit most center gemstone sizes. This ring is provided without a center gemstone. The center gemstone in this ring is sold separately.

We can make this ring perfect.

Custom gemstones? New bands? Different metal? We can do that!
