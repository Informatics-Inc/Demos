    Why use the Hold-a-Chew?

    Most dogs love rolled chew sticks because they are malleable, consumable and long-lasting. Unfortunately, dog chews can cause serious health risks. As the chew stick gets too small to hold, it goes in their mouth and is easy to swallow.

    The Hold-a-Chew is designed to prevent the dangers of choking or GI blockage caused from swallowing large pieces of dog chews. It has additional benefits as well. The Hold-a-Chew makes it easier for dogs to chew in an upright position and helps to keep their paws and legs cleaner. Dogs can also chew on the Hold-a-Chew itself because it is made of nylon polymer that will rough up, but not come apart.

    How the Hold-a-Chew Works

    Insert the Chew Works Collagen Roll into the Hold-a-Chew. Align the holes in the chew stick with the nubs of the Hold-a-Chew (for original size) and tighten securely. Re-tighten while your dog is chewing, if necessary.

    Why Collagen Chew Sticks?

    A safer and healthier alternative to rawhide, Chew Works Collagen Rolls are rawhide free, digestible, and provide joint support and a healthy skin and coat.

    Chew Works Collagen Rolls are made with 100% beef hide collagen and made in the USA with no chemicals or preservatives.
