Donation of time, talent, and treasure.

Polk County Conservation welcomes gifts of time, talent, and treasure to be used for the sustainability and enhancement of our parks and trails for present and future generations. Below are the different ways you can make a difference:

  • Donate through the Commemorative & Memorial Gift Program
    • Tribute Gift: Leave a lasting tribute in your favorite park in accordance with the values shared by the person you wish to celebrate.
    • Scholarships: Provide a child with an outdoor experience that they will remember for a lifetime!
    • Donations: Contributions of any amount can enrich people's experiences in the great outdoors.
  • Volunteer: Click here to visit our website at www.LeadingYouOutdoors.org
    • Individuals and Groups
    • Corporate Teams
  • Donate through the Great Outdoors Foundation

    The Great Outdoors Foundation is the non-profit organization that benefits Polk County Conservation. Its mission is to provide high quality, healthy living for Greater Polk County through expanded open space, restored landscapes, greater recreation, and outdoor education opportunities.
