Event Summary

The final hike will be on May 18th and take place at Chatfield Lake in Lee County. Participants should meet at the Chatfield campground parking lot at 3:00 p.m. This will be the final hike for the Spring Hike A Park series. During this series, a naturalist will lead a hike at a different county park on alternate Thursdays.

Why Attend?

Des Moines County Conservation will be partnering with Lee County Conservation for several of the hikes in this series. Whether you are trying to stay fit or want to explore some new parks, this series is for you. These programs will fluctuate between distances of 1-4 miles. Participants may leave at any time.

No Fees

There is no fee or registration required for this program. Please dress for the weather and look at the Starr’s Cave Nature Center Facebook page for event changes and cancellations or call Starr’s Cave Nature Center at (319)753-5808.
