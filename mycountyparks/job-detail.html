Seasonal Park Technician

Back to Job List
Posted 01/16/2023
County O'Brien
Location Paullina, Iowa
Salary $12-15 per hour

Description

This position includes general park maintenance and upkeep for the O'Brien County Conservation Board.

Duties & Responsibilities:

  • Clean restrooms
  • Maintain beach areas
  • Painting and staining
  • Mowing and trimming
  • Tree removal
  • Garbage collection and disposal
  • Assisting with educational programming
  • Other duties as assigned

For a full job description visit: https://prairieheritagecenter.org/about/employment-opportunities/

Qualifications

Must be 18 years of age. Must have a valid Iowa driver's license. Must pass a pre-employment physical and drug screening.

To Apply

Send or drop off resume to O’Brien County Conservation Board, 4931 Yellow Avenue, Peterson, IA 51047.
or by email: tscott@obriencounty.iowa.gov

Resumes will be accepted until the positions are filled.

More Information

https://prairieheritagecenter.org/about/employment-opportunities/
