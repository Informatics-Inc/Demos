Enjoy this Cozy Cottage in the Woods

Lake Icaria Cottages have a variety of rates that accommodate any budget and can be reserved online up to two years in advance. All online reservations (2-night minimum) must be made a minimum of 5 days prior to arrival. You may call the office to make reservations for any openings within that 5-day window or to make a week-long reservation in the off season at the discount weekly rate.

Check-In / Out Availability Check-in is after 4:00 PM and check-out is before 11:00 AM.

Location 1735 Cottage Cove Rd - From Highway 148 go East on 183rd Street.
