Developing Competitive, Winning Government Grant Applications

Federal grants can transform an organization’s reputation and capacity for impact. But the competitive stakes are high and submitting a winning proposal can be daunting.

Our Federal Grant Writing experts understand what it takes to compete for a federal grant. We will help you navigate the application process and put your organization in a stronger position to win funding.

A Proven Approach

Our team has honed techniques for assessing organizations’ most fundable needs, interpreting the decision-making patterns of funders, and finding, applying for, and winning government grants at federal, state, and local levels.

Our approach involves not just grant writing, but serving as the ultimate project manager to guide our clients through the development of complex government grant applications from the starting point to submission.

  • We create a robust project plan and hold all stakeholders accountable to key deadlines and details required for the application package.
  • We develop compelling narratives with supporting evidence aligned to the funder’s requirements, including letters of support, abstracts, logic models, and other components.
  • We finalize the completed application for your approval, and will guide you to maneuver through application portals and upload the package for submission.

The Grants Plus Difference

We approach federal grant seeking as a partnership, in which our nonprofit clients are the experts in their missions and programs, and we are the grants experts who walk them through every step of the grant application development and submission process. We bring our clients to the federal grants finish line feeling confident about their competitiveness to win funding.
