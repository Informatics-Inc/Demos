About El Cajon

If you are in need of Women's Health and Family Planning services in El Cajon, CA, FPA Women's Health is here to serve you. Our office provides a variety of services, including Cervical Cancer Screening, Annual Exam, Abortion, Women's Health, IUD, and Behavioral Health to our patients with care and expertise. FPA Women's Health has served many happy patients of the El Cajon community. We hope to see you in our office soon!

El Cajon is located in San Diego County, near the cities of Bostonia, CA and Casa de Oro-Mount Helix, CA. The city is home to a variety of schools, including Granite Hills High, and colleges such as San Diego State University and University Of Phoenix-San Diego Campus. Patients of our practice can enjoy local attractions and activities in El Cajon such as The Water Conservation Garden, Summers Past Farms, and Sycuan Casino. After your appointment, grab a bite to eat at one of El Cajon's many lively restaurants, including Marechiaro's Italian Restaurant or Antonelli's Deli.
