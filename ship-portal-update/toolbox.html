Welcome to the role-based Toolbox! This area of our website provides page content and links explaining our SHIP National Technical Assistance Center (SHIP TA Center) products and services. It also provides page content and links to other federal and national information or websites important for your role. We invite you to explore the topics and subtopic in the menu to the right or scroll down to read more information about our Center.

About the SHIP TA Center

The Center provides training, technical assistance, and promotional activities in support of the national SHIP program and its 54 individual SHIP projects. SHIP TA Center services are available for optional – not mandatory – SHIP use; however, the Center aims to serve every SHIP by providing timely, accessible, relevant, and accurate information.

If you are unsure about how your SHIP program is using the SHIP TA Center's services, contact your supervisor.

SHIP TA Center Significant Partner: The Medicare Rights Center

The Medicare Rights Center provides Medicare education, including the monthly Medicare Minutes, Online Counselor Certification and Training Tool (OCCT), newsletter articles, webinars, conference presentations, and answers to Medicare questions. The Medicare Rights Center answers SHIP questions at medicarehelp@shiptacenter.org.
