Title: COVID-19 Public Health Unwinding Materials

Edit

Description:
In 2022, the Wisconsin SHIP partnered with the Wisconsin Division of Medicaid Services to develop the following outreach materials in preparation for the unwinding of the COVID-19 public health emergency (PHE). Materials were shared during the General Enrollment Period (GEP) to a target group of Medicaid members who turned 65 during the pandemic but who were not enrolled in Medicare, encouraging them to contact Social Security to discuss Medicare enrollment.

  • GEP outreach materials to Medicaid members without Medicare
    • Mailer
    • Call Script
    • Newsletter Article
    • Social media graphics for beneficiaries aged 65+ and Social Security Disability beneficiaries
  • GEP outreach materials to Medicaid members without Medicare
    • Newsletter articles for beneficiaries aged 65+ and Social Security Disability beneficiaries 2022



In 2023, unwinding materials were updated to include information about the new Special Enrollment Period for Loss of Medicaid and unwinding timelines. Materials include:

  • Newsletter article 2023

Files:
2022 COVID unwinding newsletter articles 2022 GEP outreach to Medicaid members: Social media graphic for SSDI 2022 GEP outreach to Medicaid members: Mailer 2022 GEP outreach to Medicaid members: Call script 2023 COVID unwinding newsletter article (with new Loss of Medicaid SEP)
