2022-2023 Examination Committee Application

Apply By: Oct 05, 2021 11:59 PM

Applicants for the Examination Committee must meet the following criteria to be considered:

  1. Be an active Diplomate in good standing
  2. Board certified for at least three years
  3. Pledge to maintain a commitment to confidentiality
  4. Demonstrate contemporary knowledge in oral and maxillofacial surgical care

If your application and completed applicant evaluation forms are not received by the deadline, your application will not be considered. Please note that letters of recommendation are no longer required.

Members of the ABOMS Examination Committee perform many duties specific to the Board’s Certification and Certification Maintenance (CM) processes. All applicants are reviewed and decided at the Board’s spring meeting each year. Applicants who are accepted are recognized as Junior Examiners. Junior Examiners are required to attend an Item Writers Workshop at the AAOMS Annual Meeting and must write questions for computer based examinations and the Board’s CM program. After serving two years, Examiners are considered to examine for the Oral Certifying Examination.
