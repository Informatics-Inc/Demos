About BLS Training with Midwest Safety Partners

Led by expert instructors, our BLS training program goes beyond the basics to ensure your team is prepared for any emergency on site.

What You Will Learn

  • High-Quality CPR – Perform CPR for adults, children, and infants.
  • AED Use – Use an automated external defibrillator (AED).
  • Effective Ventilations – Deliver breaths with a barrier device.
  • AHA Chain of Survival – Understand key life-saving steps.
  • Team Dynamics – Work effectively in multi-rescuer CPR.
  • Airway Obstruction Relief – Respond to choking emergencies.

What You Can Expect

  • Convenient On-Site Training – We come to your workplace.
  • Engaging and Interactive Sessions – 1-2 hours, with active participation.
  • Personalized Attention – 1:1 manikin-to-student ratio for hands-on practice.
  • Inclusive Classes – We can offer training sessions and materials in Spanish.

Certifications & Compliance

  • Fully satisfies OSHA requirements for First Aid & CPR training.
  • Two-year certification for all participants.
  • Includes two-year access to online refresher training for continued learning.

Cost & Requirements

  • Cost: $150 per student (minimum 5 students)
  • Flexible Options: On-site or blended classes (on-site + online).
  • Custom Solutions: Ask about discounts for large groups and tailored programs.
