Lifesaving Training. Midwest Values

At Midwest Safety Partners (MSP), we’re passionate about equipping individuals and teams with the lifesaving skills they need to make a difference. Providing CPR, AED, first aid, and BLS training to corporate clients across Iowa, Illinois, and Wisconsin.

Our Roots

In 1991, Kate White Graham founded what would become Midwest Safety Partners with a simple goal: to help people save lives through quality CPR, BLS, and first aid training.

Kimberly Bramley and Keith Rippy purchased the company from Kate in 2024, introducing the MSP brand and taking our mission even further. While a lot has changed over the years, one thing remains the same—our commitment to creating safer workplaces and communities through expert, approachable safety training.

Who We Serve

We specialize in providing expert training for:

  • Corporate teams with multiple shifts and diverse needs.
  • Community organizations committed to safety and preparedness.
  • Daycare centers, schools, and other industries requiring specialized training.

What Makes Us Different

When it’s time to hire a partner to run your corporate safety training, choose the one with the right experience and capabilities.

  • Expertise serving large corporate teams with multiple shifts and diverse needs.
  • Skilled instructors with backgrounds in critical care paramedics and nursing.
  • On-site and blended learning modalities available.
  • Assistance with organization and management of safety training requirements.
  • Midwestern service and integrity at every step.
