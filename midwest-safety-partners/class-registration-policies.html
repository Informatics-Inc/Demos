Welcome to Midwest Safety Partners! Below, you'll find the rules and policies that ensure a smooth and effective learning experience for everyone participating in our workplace safety training programs.

Cancellation Policy

To cancel a course, please send an email to nstssafety@aol.com with the following details:

Your name
Class title
Class date

Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance of the class. You will receive a confirmation email, along with information about any possible refund, based on the specific cancellation policy for your course.

Discrimination Policy

Midwest Safety Partners prohibits discrimination in employment, educational programs, and activities on the basis of race, national origin, color, creed, religion, sex, age, disability, veteran status, sexual orientation, or gender identity. We are committed to fostering an inclusive and respectful environment for all participants.

Accessibility Policy

Individuals with disabilities are encouraged to attend all Midwest Safety Partners training events. If you require accommodations to participate, please contact us in advance at (319) 366-4769 to discuss your needs.

Payment Policy

All course fees must be paid in full prior to the start of the class. Accepted payment methods include credit/debit cards and company checks. Payment details will be provided during the registration process.

Certification Policy

Successful completion of a course requires active participation and demonstration of the required skills. Upon completion, participants will receive a certification valid for the specific duration outlined in the course details. Replacement certificates are available upon request for a nominal fee.

Code of Conduct

To maintain a positive and productive learning environment, participants are expected to:

  • Treat instructors and fellow participants with respect.
  • Refrain from disruptive behavior or use of inappropriate language.
  • Follow all safety guidelines and instructions provided during the training.

Failure to adhere to the Code of Conduct may result in dismissal from the course without a refund.

Thank You for Trusting
Midwest Safety Partners

Thank you for choosing Midwest Safety Partners for your safety training needs. We’re here to help you succeed and create safer workplaces and communities. For any questions about our policies, feel free to contact us at nstssafety@aol.com or (319) 366-4769.
