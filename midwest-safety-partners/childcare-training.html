Adult CPR and AED

Learn how to perform CPR and use an automated external defibrillator (AED) on adults in cardiac arrest. This course is recommended for anyone who wants to learn basic CPR skills.

Adult, Child, and Infant CPR/AED

Learn how to perform CPR on adults, infants, and children, including the use of AED and how to respond to choking emergencies. This course is recommended for parents, babysitters, and caregivers.

Standard First Aid/CPR/AED

Learn first aid care, such as treating fractures, head injuries, and allergic reactions. This course is recommended for anyone needing first aid skills for work or home. This class can be tailored to specific needs in the workplace and those who want to be prepared for more severe emergencies. We hold a special class to meet DHH requirements for childcare providers.

On-Site Training

We offer on-site CPR and first aid training for businesses, schools, and other organizations. Contact us to schedule a training session at your location.
