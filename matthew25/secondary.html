Cultivate hope Urban Farm

Matthew 25 created Iowa’s first urban farm education center in 2012.

The two-acre farm is near downtown Cedar Rapids on land that had houses that were damaged in the Flood of 2008. After the flood, the houses were torn down and we turned the then-empty lots into productive farmland.
