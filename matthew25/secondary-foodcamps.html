Summer Food Camps

Kids get to play with their food at Cultivate Hope Food Camps!

Every summer, we host camps for grades K-12 at the Cultivate Hope Urban Farm, where kids get hands-on experience learning how produce is grown and how it turns into the meals and snacks they eat every day.

Our Food Camps start with the basics for the younger campers, from learning the parts of a plant to different types of vegetables. They also use produce from the farm to make and taste healthy snacks. For the older kids, we build on this knowledge by incorporating field trips and cooking meals with produce they harvest from the Urban Farm.

No matter their age, each child learns more about how food gets to their plate!

(Grades K-2)

Food Explorers

June 9, 2021, 8:30 am – 4 pm

Do your kids love getting their hands dirty and experimenting outside? During this one-day Food Explorers Camp, your child will:

  • Learn about food through nature exploration, outdoor crafts, and scavenger hunts
  • Explore how food is grown on a farm
  • Make healthy snacks using organic veggies from the farm

Cost

$20 before April 30, $25 after.

Registration

The Food Explorers camp is currently full. Please check back early next year for 2023 registration information.

(Grades 3-5)

Food Detectives

June 14-17, 2021, 8:30 am – 4 pm

Do your kids know that food doesn’t just come from the supermarket? During this four-day Food Detectives Camp, your child will:

  • Learn about how food is grown and turns into the snacks and meals they eat every day
  • Take field trips to Old MacDonald’s Farm at Bever Park, Noelridge Greenhouse, Indian Creek Nature Center, and other food or nature-focused destinations in Cedar Rapids
  • Create snacks and a lunch using farm-fresh, organic produce.

Cost

$80 before April 30, $95 after.

Includes a T-shirt, snacks, and field trip transportation. You’ll need to bring a sack lunch Monday through Wednesday.

Registration

The Food Detectives camp is currently full. Please check back early next year for 2023 registration information.

(Grades 6-8 & 9-12)

Food Creators

June 11, 2021, 9 am – noon

Put on your chef’s hat and get creative! During this half-day camp, your student will:

  • Learn cooking skills
  • Receive a recipe
  • Find hidden ingredients around the farm
  • Compete to make the most delicious dish – the most creative, timely, AND delicious food creation wins!

Cost

$40 before April 30, $45 after.

Includes a T-shirt and the meal they will be creating.

Registration

The Food Creators camp is currently full. Please check back early next year for 2023 registration information.
