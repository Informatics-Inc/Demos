Our Focus

Building Connected, Thriving Communities

Research shows that the neighborhoods where we grow up are one of the largest indicators of our future health and success. In Cedar Rapids, there is a 10-year difference in life expectancy and a $20,000 difference in household income at age 35 in the neighborhoods Matthew 25 serves.

We’re working to close that gap through our Four Pillars of Strong Neighborhoods.
