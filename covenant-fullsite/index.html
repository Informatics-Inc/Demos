"There’s a direct correlation between workplace culture and workplace mental health. Great workplaces are committed to tapping into the unique gifts, strengths and abilities of their employees. They don’t see their people as cogs in a machine. They are are more concerned with their H2H (human to human) experiences than they are with their strategy of B2B or B2C."

James MayhewChief Culture Officer, James Mayhew Consulting

"A negative working environment may lead to physical and mental health problems, harmful use of substances or alcohol, absenteeism and lost productivity. Workplaces that promote mental health and support people with mental disorders are more likely to reduce absenteeism, increase productivity and benefit from associated economic gains."

World Health Organization

"By investing the time and effort, companies can avoid the perils of having to frequently hire and train new people, which can significantly drain resources. The benefits of addressing workplace mental health directly are widespread and impactful. Not only does it benefit the company, but it will improve workplace culture by demonstrating concern and compassion for the individual."

Jacob ChristensonCEO, Covenant Family Solutions

Our Approach

Why We Care

We exist to strengthen lives through the pursuit of wellness. We recognize that strong individuals are crucial for the wellbeing of organizations, communities, and society as a whole.

Personalized Experiences

We understand that no two individuals or organizations are the same. This is why we have developed a full suite of proven wellness solutions that can be tailored to meet those unique needs. Through seamless implementation, we help organization’s build a strong foundation of mental wellness that supports the most sustainable and engaged workplace cultures.

Measurable Results

Our programs are designed to legitimately move the needle within organizations in several ways. We make positive outcomes possible, such as increasing employee productivity, decreasing employee absenteeism and presenteeism, recruiting and retaining talented team members while reducing turnover, creating happier, more engaged employees, and so much more.
