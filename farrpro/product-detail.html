HavenTM MicroClimate Standard Controller

Optimize your farrowing environment with the push of a button.

Product PDF

Introducing the Haven, the biggest advancement in the world of farrowing technology since the heat lamp. This innovative system uses a reflective heat shield mounted above a heating bulb and flanked by curtains to create a uniform and nurturing environment for newborn piglets.

The result, as proven in a new Iowa State University study: More viable piglets and less energy use. That’s why Haven is used and trusted by high-value producers, researchers and genetics companies.

The Haven Control Box

  • Multifunction displays
  • Current heat temp under the shield
  • Heating icon or idle status
  • Easy recovery from power loss
  • Automatically senses the temperature under the shield
  • Maintains an ideal temperature
  • 28-day cycle with predetermined temperature control
