Our Story

Committed to Collaboration

Founded in 2017 as a startup at the University of Iowa, FarrPro has grown into a national company counting some of the top pork producers in the United States as customers.

Today, you’ll find our microclimate and data solutions everywhere from high-value animal research and genetics facilities to high-efficiency farrowing and finishing rooms.

And we’re just getting started.
