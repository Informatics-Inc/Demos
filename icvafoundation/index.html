Advancing Healthcare for Our Nation's Heroes

The Iowa City VA Medical Research Foundation mobilizes resources that help target, cure, and improve veterans' health problems.

Donate Today

Serving veterans in Iowa and Illinois for improved health solutions.

We are based in Iowa City, a hub of research and health care in the Midwest.

ICVARF is a nonprofit corporation founded to help the Iowa City VA Health Care System advance its healthcare goals. We support medical research projects led by VA staff in order to assist and improve the healthcare provided to the veteran community.

We welcome you to learn more about our organization, including our board members, staff and researchers and the medical research being conducted through our support. To find out how we serve our nation's heroes by funding medical research, please feel free to Contact Us.
