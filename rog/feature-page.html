Impact Investing & Consulting

We Help You Create the Greatest Impact with Your Investment

Return on Good guides you through a careful process to have the best impact with your dollars. We understand that strategic investing creates an ideal opportunity to identify and measure positive social, economic, or environmental impact alongside your financial return.

Once you choose the nonprofits, startups or similar organizations that best match your values, core mission and giving style, the next steps toward giving can feel arduous. But not when you allow ROG to facilitate your impact investment. We handle the often challenging, uncomfortable and laborious work behind the scenes, saving you time to focus on what’s most important: the joy of giving.

  • Maximize giving power with ROG's expertise.
  • Unleash philanthropy's full potential through meticulous planning.
  • Gain peace of mind with our comprehensive handling of your investment.
  • Empower your vision as we manage details and recognition.
  • Collaborate to amplify impact with key stakeholders.
  • Be in control of your philanthropic mission with ROG.

Return on Impact Investing

With Return on Good, you’re in the driver’s seat of your philanthropic mission. Always.

When you turn to ROG for help with impact investing, we do thorough research and planning. We request data from your selected organizations and share it with you so you can have peace of mind about your giving investment. Essentially, we take care of everything, from confidential inquiry to plans for recognition and stewardship.

Each step of the way, we listen to you and honor your wishes. We talk with those you want us to talk with, from family members to board members and financial planners to current receiving organizations. With Return on Good, you’re in the driver’s seat of your philanthropic mission. Always.

Our Facilited Giving/Impact Investing Consultive Process

A Collaborative Journey: Guiding Your Impact Investing with Expert Facilitation

  • We make the initial confidential contacts with your best-fit nonprofits
  • We share your mission and develop potential impact investing opportunities, anonymously if preferred
  • We gauge the potential recipient’s interest, quality, and capacity to meet your investing goals
  • We secure alignment before you meet to ensure comfortable conversations
  • We produce signed contracts, covering everything from gift use to recognition expectations to continued stewardship

Return on Good Impact Investment Consulting Includes:

  • A full record of your giving and service impact to date
  • A giving mission statement outlining your values and the impact you wish to make
  • ROG Research Reports that explore key charities you’re considering for investment
  • Facilitated conversations with charities you’re considering
  • Preserving your anonymity until you’re ready to be known
  • Charity site visits and exploration as you prepare to invest
  • Development of the ideal giving plan with the charities you choose

Case Study

Lumicell

The Challenge: To create a marketing and fundraising strategy to raise $50M+ for the second phase of an innovative cancer detection solution

An innovative tech company that illuminates residual breast cancer cells intra-operatively to assist in the detection of cancerous tissue following removal of the primary specimen during lumpectomy surgery. This life-saving technology is poised to change the landscape for cancer surgeries.

Research in the New England Journal of Medicine touts this as a promising breast cancer surgery intervention which can lead to further use-cases. Return on Good has been retained to curate a list of potential investors, refine materials for the pitch and develop strategies for private investment meetings.

Learn more at: Lumincell.com

Case Study

WellnessWits

The Challenge: To fund the development and go to market strategy of a healthcare tech platform raising $5M to bring the product to market and fulfill contracts in motion.

WellnessWits connects patients to an ecosystem of health experts and peer support groups with targeted therapeutic programs designed to make it easier to improve health and wellbeing outcomes with as little effort as possible.

This new, socially-connected health solution lowers healthcare costs and boosts patient results through group therapeutics and tech-enabled success tools. Return on Good has been retained to curate investment marketing collateral and help secure principal investors.

Learn more at: WelnessWits.com
