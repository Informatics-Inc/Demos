Strategic Philanthropy

We help our clients define, reveal and manifest their values to maximize the good they do in the world.

Working with Return on Good helps:

Establish Structure & Aligned Process for Giving

We are equipped to help Family offices that are ready to establish a Donor Advised Fund or a Foundation and have an idea of the amount and benefits, but do not yet have a clear vision on how they want to distribute funds, or work through the process.

Create a Strategic Philanthropy Plan Focused on your Passion

When a family has decided that they would like to be more intentional in charitable giving. The focus is on leaving a legacy and creating a long term strategy that involves all family members, aligning goals around their passion areas and big projects.

Capture Digital Memories and Giving Stories

For individuals who want to honor the work that their family or foundation has done, we create legacy videos that can be presented as a gift. We capture video interviews from those recipients of their philanthropic giving, joined with interviews with the family.

How We Work with Clients

Each client is a unique strategy based on their family and priorities.

Through an in-depth discovery process, we truly get to know and understand the dynamics of your family and passions. With a clear framework, we assist in moving to finished plans.
