So what is NoDoz?

NoDoz is an alertness aid that helps restore mental alertness and wakefulness when you’re feeling fatigued or drowsy. From late nights, to long drives, to leg day, NoDoz is the perfect pick-me-up for when you need that little extra push to keep you going.

What are the active ingredients in NoDoz?

Charisma, motivation, all the answers… well, not exactly. The only active ingredient in NoDoz is caffeine, 200mg per pill to be exact, which is about how much you’ll find in 1 to 2 cups of coffee.

Affordable, convenient, and calorie free, NoDoz is the ideal choice for on-the-go energy. Check out our product comparison page to see how NoDoz stacks up against caffeine’s heaviest hitters, including energy drinks, coffee, and energy shots.

What makes NoDoz different from other caffeine pills?

NoDoz knows that caffeine is about more than just energy. It’s about bringing out the qualities that make you great. It’s about getting stuff done and making things happen. NoDoz gives you 200mg of the caffeine you need to win your day, every day.

Can I take NoDoz?

NoDoz is only intended for adults and children 12 years and over. Children under the age of 12 should not take NoDoz. If you are pregnant or breastfeeding, have any medical conditions, or are taking any medications, talk to your doctor before using NoDoz. It’s important to know how caffeine affects you, so we always recommend talking to your doctor before use if you have any questions or concerns.

When and how should I take NoDoz?

Always use NoDoz as directed, limiting your intake to ½ to 1 pill every 3-4 hours. It’s important to keep track of your caffeine consumption and limit the use of other foods, beverages, and supplements containing caffeine while taking NoDoz. Remember, NoDoz is for occasional use only and is not a substitute for sleep.

How much caffeine is in NoDoz?

One NoDoz pill contains 200mg of caffeine, or roughly the equivalent of 1-2 cups of coffee.
