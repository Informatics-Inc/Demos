About FPA Women's Health of Berkeley

If you are in need of Women's Health and Family Planning services in Berkeley, CA, FPA Women's Health is here to serve you. Our office provides a variety of services, including Cervical Cancer Screening, Behavioral Health, STI Testing, Abortion, Women's Health, and Behavioral Health to our patients with care and expertise. FPA Women's Health has served many happy patients of the Berkeley community. We hope to see you in our office soon!

Berkeley is located in Alameda County, near the cities of Albany, CA and Kensington, CA. The city is home to a variety of schools, including Berkeley High, and colleges such as Laney College and College Of Alameda. Patients of our practice can enjoy local attractions and activities in Berkeley such as University of California, Berkeley, Tilden Regional Park, and Tilden Park Little Farm. After your appointment, grab a bite to eat at one of Berkeley's many lively restaurants, including Berkeley Bowl or Cheeseboard Pizza.
