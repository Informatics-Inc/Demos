About Infusions

Effective and Efficient Treatment

IV therapy is an efficient way to treat chronic conditions, delivering treatment into the bloodstream without continuous needle-pricking or orally administered medication. The body absorbs medication at a higher rate through this method, providing more immediate and powerful relief.

Experienced nurse practitioners with specialized skills can administer IV therapy in the home or at outpatient centers. This streamlined approach allows patients to receive ongoing treatment, minimizing disruptions to their daily lives.

Note that Infusion services are initiated by physician referral. Patients seeking infusion services should discuss treatment with their doctor.
