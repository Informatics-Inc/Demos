Patient benefits

Your patients are in good hands

Made up of pharmacists, pharmacy techs, and nurses, our clinical staff is here to provide direction and instruction. We get to know each patient, offering compassion and ensuring comfort.

Specialized expertise

At hospitals and clinics, infusion care is one of many services offered. By contrast, our team members are specifically trained in compounding and administering specialized medications. Nurses hired here complete a lengthy onboarding process, ensuring they’re fully trained and comfortable before delivering infusions before care.

Learn More

Comfort & privacy

Many of our patients come to us with chronic, uncomfortable conditions. It’s our priority to make infusion center patients feel relaxed, comfortable, and cared for. While they receive treatment, patients enjoy complimentary Wi-Fi and personal tablets. They may also opt for a private suite.

Learn More

Customized Treatments

We work with you to create individualized care plans. From there, our nurses manage symptoms, conduct ongoing therapy assessments, and collaborate to make any necessary adjustments.

Learn More

Home Infusions

For a variety of reasons, patients choose to have us come to them. From the comfort of their homes, we can provide the training and education needed for self-administered or caregiver-administered treatment. We check in with our home infusion patients in person at least once a week.
