Infusion Solutions for Patients

From the moment of referral, we strive to achieve the best possible outcome for every patient by focusing on quality, affordability, and comfort. Infusion services are available in the patient’s home or at a nearby infusion center location.

Infusion centers

Comfortable Treatment Spaces

Patients can sit back and relax during treatment, as each of our treatment centers is designed to provide the most pleasant experience possible. They include:

  • Private infusion suites
  • TVs and tablets
  • High-speed internet
  • Comfortable seating

Home infusions

A Convenient Care Option

The home infusion approach supports a patient’s lifestyle and schedule with minimal disruption. For some, it’s a cost-effective and liberating alternative to an extended stay in the hospital or nursing facility.

Home infusion services

  • Individualized, in-person training by a specialized nurse
  • Weekly nursing visists
  • Medication and supply delivery
  • Access to our 24-hour support

THE PATIENT PROCESS

Getting Started with Us

Patients Consult Providers

Typically, care providers identify when someone will benefit from infusion treatment. However, patients can initiate the conversation by asking the provider if there are any infusion or injectable therapies that may help manage or improve their condition.

Learn More

Providers prescribe to our pharmacy

After discussing treatment options with the patient, the care provider will prescribe a medication and submit the order to our pharmacy.

Learn More

Our pharmacy authorizes the prescription

At this point, our pharmacists will collaborate with the patient’s physician. We validate the patient’s details, confirm their benefits, and secure the necessary insurance authorization for the prescribed medication.

Learn More

We Reach Out

We will initiate the reach out to the patient, working with them to schedule an appointment at one of our Washington infusion center locations or at home.

Learn More

Patients Receive Care

Before treatment takes place, one of our nurses will educate the patient and answer any questions. Then, they will administer infusions, draw labs, and care for the patient while treatment is ongoing.

Learn More

Questions about the patient process?
Both pharmacists and nurses are on call 24/7 to answer patient questions and resolve concerns.
