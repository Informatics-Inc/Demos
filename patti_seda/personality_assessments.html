The Tools

Discover Your Authentic Self

Short for Management & Performance in Organizations, the MPO Personality Assessment empowers leaders and managers with data to help them align and strengthen teams. By understanding how a person is authentically wired, you can ensure they are in the right seat with your organization and play to their strengths. People who aren’t aligned in a role with their strengths are more frustrated and become disengaged.

Watch “CHARTACTER FLAW OR STRENGTH” video.

The MPO Profile describes seven different respondent personality traits, including five major personality dimensions common to all people, known by psychologists as the “Big Five.” It takes just 15 minutes to complete, and generates in-depth insights on a person’s specific:

  • Leadership Style
  • Communication Style
  • Preferred Work Environment
  • Motivational Needs & Factorse
  • Decision-Making Process
  • Level of Adaptability
  • And More

The EQ-I 2.0 Emotional Intelligence Assessment

This online self-assessment is considered the leading measure of emotional intelligence, with decades of research to back it up. The EQ-i 2.0 provides a detailed assessment of a person’s emotional strengths and areas for development as it relates to the workplace and leadership effectiveness.

This assessment provides an analysis of your strengths in 15 separate EQ competencies, and classifies users through five EQ composite areas:

  • Self-Perception
  • Self-Expression
  • Interpersonal
  • Decision Making
  • Stress Management

Becoming a Certified Analyst

Organizations that implement MPO Personality Assessments experience more respectful communication and stronger teamwork.

Companies can select an on-site “certified analyst,” who receives further training on the MPO platform and facilitates the testing of new team members. These in-house certified analysts can deliver on the promise to allow employees to be their authentic selves, while providing strategies for better communication and teamwork.
