How to maximize your time in our salon

Back to Blog List

Last week, Chris Linford from Beauty as a Business led an Education Episode called How to Dominate on Google with a Salon Suite. This class walked our Members through how to get a lot of Google Reviews the right way, why an online reputation with Google is important to your bottom line, and how to maximize being found on Google to attract new clients and ultimately increase revenue in your business. Below are some of the topics and key takeaways from the class.

Good vs. Great Marketing:

Good marketing is talking about your business on social media. GREAT marketing is getting other people to talk about your business. Don’t wait for it to happen naturally. Sometimes, you need to get awkward. Ask your clients while they are sitting in the chair to leave a Google review or post about your business.

Get your clients to share their experience on their social media. Friends and family members of your clients will see that and that is who you want to get in front of. They are 4x more likely to book with you if they are a referral and they will spend 18% more dollars.

Building your brand

How much of somebody’s mind is already made up before they call or inquire about any business? 70% What are you doing online to have an impact on that 70%? You need to build trust.
