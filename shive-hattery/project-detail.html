HealthLinc Sturdy Road Medical Office Building Renovations

Located in Marion, IA

contributors

First Lastname Director of Greatness, Shive-Hattery
First Lastname Senior Engineer, Shive-Hattery

Increased Functionality + More Appealing To Visitors

Red Cedar Lodge increased in actual usable space. It now meets current building codes + ADA requirements. The lodge gained a fresh new look while maintaining its natural beauty.

After analyzing what was required by current codes and the improvements wanted by Linn County Conservation, the design team found that a renovation would not leave much of the old building and would cost about the same as constructing a new building. It was decided to tear down the lodge and build a new one in the same location.

ADA-equipped restrooms are located near the front, where a larger entry vestibule is able to greet visitors more easily than the previous cramped entry. In-floor heat and air conditioning were added, as well as more updated finishes throughout.

“I love how the high clerestory windows were incorporated into the design to emit natural lighting + ground-level windows included to create panoramic views of the surrounding park.”
