Credentialing Core Features

We understand your work, which is why the Credentialing Core includes the most essential certification management functions needed by high-performing organizations like yours. You won’t find this breadth of offerings anywhere else on the market.

  • Program Information Access: Users can easily access program details, prerequisites, and application forms.
  • Comprehensive People Management: Provides extensive management of pre and post-certification candidates.
  • Application Management: Facilitates application submission with required fields, progress saving, and document capture for eligibility documentation.
  • Registration Management: Efficiently handles multiple registration periods, deadlines, and extensions.
  • Automated Communications: Enhances engagement with automated emails, SMS, and portal notifications, providing alerts for deadlines and eligibility, and guiding ineligible candidates towards future eligibility.
  • Status Tracking: Allows candidates to monitor application and certification/recertification progress.
  • Staff Functionality: Merges duplicate records, manages passwords, segment groups, sets up certification logic, and create automated workflows.
  • Attributes: Tag individuals manually or automatically within workflows, granting or restricting access to system functionalities, ensuring effective account management and user control.
  • Products: Set up products in the system, collect payments, and report on sales.
  • Quality Assurance: Includes a randomized audit feature.
  • Recertification Management: Tracks continuing education requirements and offers support for CE reporting.
  • Configurable Workflows: Configure custom workflows, certification cycles, and requirements without coding.
  • Customized Branding: Integrate your organization’s brand within the software.
  • Payments Management: Process, track, and report on various fees.
  • Reporting: Access and export comprehensive on-demand reports.
